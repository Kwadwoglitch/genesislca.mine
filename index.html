About Us

Genesis Legal Consult and Associates is a client-oriented legal services firm operating in the United States of America, the United Kingdom, Canada, and Ghana. Established as an unlimited company in 2016 and registered under Lampo Empire, the firm specializes in contemporary business law in the USA, Ghana, Canada and Britain. It provides comprehensive advocacy and litigation services, as well as consultation on all aspects of business development law in these regions. The founding partners bring extensive experience from both legal and non-legal sides of corporate and commercial practice, with significant expertise in both public and private sectors. The firm's clientele includes high-profile individuals and institutions in finance, marketing, design, and engineering across Ghana, Britain, the USA, Canada and internationally. Genesis Legal Consult and Associates prides itself on being a central hub for effective, integrated project development.
